Islamic wills and legacies

Islam has placed a great emphasis on distributing the wealth of Muslims in a pre-determined manner before death.

It is a duty of a Muslim who has anything to bequeath not to let two nights pass without including it in his Will.

(Hadith)
By far the best way of ensuring that your assets are distributed correctly upon death, is to prepare a legallyvalid Will. You can benefit society even after your passing and gain an ongoing reward of Sadaqah Jariyah by leaving a bequest to charity. If you'd like to discuss leaving a gift to Muslim Hands then please do get in touch with us. Read more about preparing a Will and leaving a bequest to charity in the guide below.
