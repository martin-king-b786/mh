Hunger

The extreme form of poverty

The Problem

This is no easy cliché. When you do not havefood, you have hit rock bottom on the scale of poverty. Hunger is a wide term and has many consequences for communities who do not have access to sufficient food supplies. Whether it's following a famine, as a result of conflict or a simple lack of resources, Muslim Hands understands the varied approach needed to alleviate the consequences of hunger and tackle its root causes.

The solution out of this state of constant suffering is as simple as it is vital: providing nutritious food for the hungry. For all these reasons, food distribution has always been a focal part of Muslim Hands' work. We want to help you help those who are desperate for the simple joy of a plateful of hot food and a happy family that is free from thedesperation of hunger.
