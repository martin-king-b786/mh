Safe Water

The fight for drops of life

It's easy to forget how much we need safe water. This is the essential of life which many are denied access to. For over a billion people, life's most pressing challenge is gaining easy access to safe, clean drinking water. Poor health due to unclean water and conflict surrounding scarce water sources prevents many from escaping the poverty trap.

No intervention has greater overall impact upon national development and public health than the provision of safe drinking water and the proper disposal of human waste.

(World Health Organisation)

The link between unsafe water and poverty

Water

drinking contaminated water makes children and adults sick

Illness

half of the world hospital beds are occupied by patients suffering from water related diseases

Poverty

having sick adults means a loss of labour and wages, making poor situations difficult to survive

School

sick children result in them staying home, meaning they are unable to complete school
