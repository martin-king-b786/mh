Direct Debit Guarantee

This guarantee is offered by all banks and building societies that take part in the Direct Debit scheme. The efficiency and security of the scheme is monitored by your own bank or building society. If the amount to be paid or the payment date changes Muslim Hands will notify you 5 working days in advance of your account being debited, or as otherwise agreed. If an error is made by Muslim Hands or your bank or building society, you are guaranteed a full and immediate refund from your branch of the amount paid. You cancel a Direct Debit at any time by writing to your bank or building society or by contact Muslim Hands.
